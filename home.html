All of our business solicitors are experienced and commercially minded, taking time to understand the issues specific to you, whether you run an owner managed business or a large PLC.

 

SME Solicitors will provide you with direct contact details for your solicitor and their assistant, giving you peace of mind that when you need us, we are here to help.

Why Use SME Solicitors?

 

At SME Solicitors we are a refreshingly different, well established and forward thinking legal firm based in Worcester. We have an enviable history dating back to 1840.

We believe in offering a dedicated and personal service to resolve your individual requirements. We do that through a solid foundation of traditional methods with modern practices.

Our prime objective is to build a relationship with you whereby we not only address your current requirements, but we are able to anticipate for the future too.

When you appoint us to look after your affairs you can be assured that the solicitor you appoint will be looking after you personally, from the start to finish.

Contact us today on 01905 723561 or by email at info@smesolicitors.co.uk and experience our friendly approach.
