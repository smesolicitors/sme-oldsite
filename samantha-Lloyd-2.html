Name: Samantha Lloyd, Partner – Head of Private Client

Department: Private Client

Email: samantha.lloyd@smesolicitors.co.uk

Phone Number:  01905 723561

LinkedIn: https://www.linkedin.com/in/samantha-lloydsme/ 

 

Samantha is a solicitor and Head of the Private Client Department.

 

She qualified in 2000 and after 10 years of practicing in Birmingham has worked for the last 13 years in Worcester. Samantha has also achieved the highly regarded STEP qualification standing her out for her expertise.

 

Samantha deals with all probate matters working closely with bereaved families supporting them through this difficult time.

 

Samantha specialises in wills, probate and estate administration, capacity issues including, powers of attorney, Deputyship applications and Trust administration.
